About Don Norman

Don Norman is a pioneer in the field of Human–Computer Interaction, known for his work on user-centered design, usability, and cognitive engineering.

Why He Matters in This Course

Norman’s Action Cycle helps us understand how users form goals, execute actions, and evaluate system feedback. This model is essential for comparing cognitive interaction theories such as GOMS and KLM.
